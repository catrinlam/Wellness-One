Mental Health Information

Mental health is a state of well-being in which an individual realises their own potential, copes with the normal stresses of life, works productively, and contributes to their community.

  • Anxiety Disorders
  • Depression
  • Stress
  • Bipolar Disorder
  • Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)

Recognising the early signs of mental health issues can make a significant difference. Look out for:
  • Changes in sleeping or eating patterns
  • Persistent feelings of sadness or irritability
  • Withdrawal from friends and activities
  • Difficulty concentrating

  • Maintain a balanced diet and regular exercise
  • Practice mindfulness or meditation
  • Stay connected with friends and family
  • Set realistic goals and prioritise tasks

Stress Management Tips

Here are some simple, practical techniques you can try to help manage stress in your daily life.

  • Practice deep breathing when feeling stressed.
  • Take breaks to clear your mind.
  • Use time management techniques to stay organised.
  • Stay connected with friends and family to share your feelings and reduce stress.
  • Try mindfulness or meditation to focus on the present moment and relax.
  • Regular physical activity can improve your mood. Aim for 150 minutes of activity weekly, or start with short walks or local group activities.
  • Speak to your GP about tailored exercise programmes, which may be free or discounted.

Resources

Here are some trusted organisations and helplines you can contact for more help and support.

Mind.org

A UK charity offering support and information on mental health.

Samaritans

A 24/7 helpline providing confidential support for those in emotional distress.

NHS

Access mental health support and resources from the UK National Health Service.

If it's an emergency or you need urgent help

  • If you or someone else is in danger, call 999 or go to A&E now.
  • If you need urgent help for your mental health, get help from NHS 111 online or call 111.
Your mental health is as important as your physical health. You will not be wasting anyone's time.
